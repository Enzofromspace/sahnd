Sahnd Help

Tools

  • Stick (key 1): fine line brush for detail work.
  • Finger (key 2): adds sand and smears it in the drag direction.
  • Trowel (key 3): digs sand out and pushes it downward.
  • Brush Size (keys [ and ]): scales the active tool from 0.5x to 2.5x.

Sand Color

  • Click a palette swatch to use that color instantly.
  • Use the picker or type a hex code (press Enter) for custom colors.
  • Lock to AGI palette snaps custom colors to the 16 EGA colors.

Backdrop

  • Pick a scene image, or None for a blank canvas.
  • Scene Tint colors the whole backdrop (white = no tint).

Scene

  • Fill sets how much sand a Reset starts with (Empty to High).
  • Reset (key R): clears to the selected fill amount.
  • Undo (Ctrl/Cmd+Z): steps back up to 10 strokes or resets.
  • Save: downloads the canvas as a PNG.

Audio

  • Music and SFX have independent toggles and volume sliders.
  • Audio starts after your first click (browser autoplay rules).
